WASD rotate the camera.
QE roll the camera.
Arrow keys move forward, backwards and sideways.
Left control lowers you.
Z pushes out the warp ball.
X reels in the warp ball.
Space warps you to the warp ball.
Press M to bring up the miniworld minimap
C toggles between 3rd person monocam and first person bicam
L triggers the animated walk through. Do not move at that time because the parent's position is
not locked.
Once the red beam is on the object, the object can be rotated by dragging it with the mouse. Some things don't rotate super nicely.
The trees and the globe can be rotated.
